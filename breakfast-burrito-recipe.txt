Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 1/2 cup of salsa
 - 2 flour tortilla shells
 - 1/3 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
 - 1/3 cup of bacon bits

Directions:
 1. Preheat skillet to 350 degrees F.
 
 2. Scramble eggs in skillet until gold and brown.
 
 3. Add eggs, salsa and bacon bits to tortillas.
 
 4. fold tortillas into burrito shape.
